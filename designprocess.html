Project Title
The Design Process: Explained Video Series
Date
2018-2019
Type
Video Production, Editing, Animation
Programs
About

A video series created for the industrial design course at Swinburne. The seven part video series aims to teach students how to bring their design from the research, ideation, refinement, all the way to selling it. I storyboarded, shot, edited and animated the series while at the Bureau design studio.
