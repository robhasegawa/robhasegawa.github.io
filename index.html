Ambient Looper: Music For Airports

ABOUT

This project aims to bring Brian Eno’s classic album ‘Ambient 1: Music for Airports’ to its original goal, a generative album that composes a random piece of music from a set of melodies. My version is still quite simple, and there are future plans to add more layers to the music. Until then, enjoy the relaxing piano music!
